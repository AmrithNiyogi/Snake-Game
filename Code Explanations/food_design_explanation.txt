Imports: Import the Shape class from the turtle module.

FoodDesign class:

    __init__ method: Initializes the FoodDesign object with a reference to the screen and calls the method to register the custom apple shape.

    register_apple_shape method:

        Creates a compound shape for the apple.

        Defines the coordinates for the different components of the apple (body, stem, and leaf).

        Adds these components to the compound shape with their respective colors.

        Registers the custom apple shape with the screen.
